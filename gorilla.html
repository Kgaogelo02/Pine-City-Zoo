ANIMALS MAP PLACES

GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest living primates and are found in forests across central Africa. They live in groups led by a dominant male known as a silverback.

Gorillas are strong but gentle and intelligent, capable of using tools and showing emotions. They are primarily herbivores and spend most of their time foraging and resting.
