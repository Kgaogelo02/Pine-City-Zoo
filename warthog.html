WARTHOG

Warthogs are wild pigs native to Africa, recognized by their large tusks and warty faces. Despite their looks, they are fast and surprisingly agile.

They live in burrows and prefer open plains. Warthogs often kneel on their front legs to graze and can defend themselves fiercely when threatened.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the warthogs at pen W18, in the grasslands area.
