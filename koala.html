ANIMALS MAP PLACES

KOALA

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia. They are known for their fluffy ears, large noses, and their diet of eucalyptus leaves.

Koalas sleep up to 20 hours a day due to their low-energy diet and spend most of their lives in trees. They are a symbol of Australian wildlife.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the koalas at pen K12, near the Australian Outback exhibit.
