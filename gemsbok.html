GEMSBOK

The gemsbok is a large antelope native to arid regions of Southern Africa. It is known for its striking appearance, with long, straight horns and bold black-and-white facial markings.

Gemsbok are highly adapted to desert life and can survive without water for long periods by extracting moisture from the plants they eat. They typically live in herds and are known for their strength and ability to defend themselves from predators.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the gemsbok at pen A14, near the desert biome exhibit.
