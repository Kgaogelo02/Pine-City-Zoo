ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY

Monkeys are highly social and intelligent animals. They are known for their agility, curiosity, and communication skills.

They live in trees and use their tails and limbs to swing and jump. Different species of monkeys are found in tropical forests around the world.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the monkeys at pen M07, near the tree canopy trail.
