PANDA

Pandas are native to China and are known for their black-and-white fur and love of bamboo. They are a symbol of wildlife conservation efforts worldwide.

Although bears, they are mostly herbivores. Pandas are shy, gentle, and spend most of their day eating and resting.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the pandas at pen P22, beside the bamboo grove.
